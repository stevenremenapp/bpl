Stop #7

Around the corner you'll see a walkway from the renovated area to the Grand Hall. Look up and you'll notice it says "Children's Entrance" above the door -- this used to be an entrance into the library, and yes, you guessed it, it was for children. Walk through here and find the Harry Allen Room, which is where we keep our local history items.

Say hi to Martha Baldwin, this library's founder, as you walk in and find your code on the newly restored window. It's said that her ghost still haunts this building.
